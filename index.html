Daily Balance Puzzle
The Challenge
Balance all 10 balls perfectly across two plates of a scale — so both sides weigh exactly the same.
How to Play
  • Drag exactly 5 balls to the Left plate and 5 to the Right
  • Tap Weigh It once all 10 balls are placed
  • Bigger circles are heavier — weight equals the circle's area, not its diameter
3 Attempts Per Day
  • After attempts 1 and 2 you'll learn how many balls are on the correct side — but not which ones
  • Only a perfect split wins: Perfectly Poised
  • A new puzzle every day
